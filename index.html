How to Protect Your Privacy

  • Use a VPN to mask your IP address and location.
  • Browse in private or incognito mode to prevent tracking.
  • Disable third-party cookies and limit data sharing permissions.
  • Use privacy-focused browsers with extensions that block tracking.
  • Keep your software and browser updated to avoid vulnerabilities.
